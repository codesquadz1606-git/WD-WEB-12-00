What is List in HTML?
List are of two types:
1. Ordered 
    Where ordered is denoted by ol 
    ol tag is a parent tag & child tag is called li(list item) tag

    ol are of 5 types : 1,i,I,A,a 

2. Unordered
    Where unordered is denoted by ul
    ul tag is parent tag & child tag is called li

    ul are of three types : square, circle, disc

What is Linking in HTML?
Linking means linking any url or a internal page .

a tag : anchor tag
