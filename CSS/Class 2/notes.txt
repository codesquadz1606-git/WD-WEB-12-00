Selectors in CSS :

class is denoted by . and is a symbol of common elements.
tagname is denoted by tagname , here style is going to apply on all the same tags.
universal is denoted by * which means applied on whole body

Positions : 5 types
1. Static (Default) : In this we cannot move any object from its place.
2. Absolute : In this it will move from its parent space & leaves his space.
3. Relative : In this it will move from its own space , and do not leave his space..
4. Fixed : Here the element stick at one place and leave his space.
5. Sticky : Here Position is stick to one place but do not leaves his space
